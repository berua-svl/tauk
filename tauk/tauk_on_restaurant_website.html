Our Story
Georgian Energy.
Street Food Soul.

TAUK ON was born on the streets of Tbilisi — a city where food isn't just fuel, it's culture, community, and conversation. We took everything we love about Georgian street food and gave it a modern, fast-food edge.

Every burger, every pizza, every shawarma is made fresh — never frozen, never rushed (well, fast, but fresh). We source local ingredients, work with local suppliers, and believe that fast food should feel like a real meal.

"TAUK ON" means "keep going" in our team's language — and that's the energy we bring every single day.
